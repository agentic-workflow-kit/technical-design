# Outcome Studies

Outcome studies compare approved designs with downstream delivery friction. They are manual and
periodic; they do not directly rewrite rubrics or gates.

Raw evidence should stay outside committed fixtures unless it has been redacted and reviewed.

## Signals

- Planner clarifications.
- Implementation blockers.
- Ownership, invariant, or enforcement review findings.
- Rework after implementation starts.
- Test strategy gaps.

## Promotion Rule

A finding can become a deterministic fixture, judge criterion, or rubric change only when it has:

- repeated source-grounded evidence;
- human review;
- a concrete target fixture or criterion;
- no private details in committed artifacts.
